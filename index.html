Services

"Transform any space into an oasis you can be proud of!"

Garden Design and Creation: Lucky Shrub excels in designing and bringing to life personalized garden spaces that reflect the individual tastes and lifestyles of their clients. Their team of skilled designers works closely with customers to develop customized plans, ensuring each garden becomes a one-of-a-kind retreat. From traditional and classic designs to contemporary and innovative concepts, they showcase remarkable versatility in garden design.

Maintenance: Beyond the initial design and creation, Lucky Shrub is dedicated to the ongoing care and nurturing of the gardens they craft. They provide comprehensive maintenance services, tending to every aspect of the garden to ensure its sustained health and vibrancy. Their expert horticulturists ensure that plants, trees, and shrubs flourish, allowing the garden to evolve and thrive.

Landscaping: Lucky Shrub transforms outdoor spaces into captivating landscapes that seamlessly integrate with their surroundings. They specialize in the construction of hardscape elements such as pathways, patios, and water features, enhancing both the functionality and aesthetics of the garden. Known for their attention to detail and commitment to quality, their landscaping projects stand out for their beauty and durability.

About
Based in Tuscon, Arizona, Lucky Shrub is a medium-sized garden design firm that specializes in garden design and creation, maintenance and landscaping. The company also runs a small plant nursery that sells indoor and outdoor plants, making them a one-stop shop for clients. Lucky Shrub was started by a husband and wife team, Jason and Maria, who share a long-time love for plants. Jason is the garden architect. He creates and oversees all designs while managing his team of landscapers. Maria manages all the marketing for the company and oversees the nursery. Lucky Shrub is a respected medium-sized garden design firm renowned for its expertise in crafting stunning outdoor spaces through garden design and creation, meticulous maintenance, and imaginative landscaping. With a commitment to creating unique and thriving gardens, Lucky Shrub has become a trusted name in the industry.

Lucky Shrub is committed to sustainability and environmentally responsible practices. They incorporate native plants and eco-friendly materials into their designs and landscaping projects, ensuring that their work contributes positively to the environment and local ecosystem. With a passion for enhancing outdoor beauty and functionality, Lucky Shrub has earned a reputation for excellence in garden design, maintenance, and landscaping. Whether it's creating an oasis of tranquility, preserving the natural charm of a garden, or crafting an inviting outdoor gathering space, they bring creativity and expertise to every project. Lucky Shrub is the go-to choice for garden enthusiasts seeking distinctive, thriving, and sustainable outdoor spaces.
